Dance Forms We Teach

Hip Hop

Embrace the dynamic and expressive world of Hip Hop. Our classes cover a range of styles including popping, locking, breaking, and freestyle, emphasizing rhythm, creativity, and musicality.

Ballet

Discover the grace and discipline of Ballet. Our classes focus on classical techniques, fostering strength, flexibility, and elegance. Perfect for both beginners and advanced dancers, Ballet at Steps & Styles enhances your poise and artistic expression.

Classical Dance

Immerse yourself in the rich traditions of Classical Dance. Our curriculum includes a variety of classical dance styles, promoting discipline, technique, and cultural understanding.

Contemporary Dance

Unleash your creativity with Contemporary Dance. This style combines elements of modern dance, jazz, and ballet, encouraging fluid movement and emotional expression. It’s ideal for dancers looking to tell stories and convey emotions through their performance.

Salsa

Feel the rhythm and passion of Salsa. Our Salsa classes are vibrant and energetic, focusing on partner work, footwork, and musical interpretation. It’s a fun way to improve coordination and social dancing skills.

Jazz

Experience the high-energy and stylistic flair of Jazz. Our classes incorporate sharp movements, isolations, and leaps, set to a variety of music styles. Jazz dance is great for building strength, flexibility, and stage presence.
